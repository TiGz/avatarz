# Add Fantasy Styles to Avatarz

## Overview

Expand the fantasy category with 20+ new styles based on research from Gemini Pro 3/Nano Banana Pro prompts. The current fantasy category has only 4 styles (fantasy, cyberpunk, mecha-anime, monochrome-manga) while other categories like animated (11) and fun (11) are much more populated.

## Problem Statement

The fantasy category is under-represented with only 4 styles. Users looking for fantasy-themed avatar transformations have limited options. Research shows there's significant demand for fantasy portrait styles including:

- Medieval fantasy (knights, warriors)
- High fantasy (elves, wizards, magical creatures)
- Dark fantasy (vampires, necromancers, gothic)
- Mythological (gods, angels, demons)
- Creature transformations (dragons, werewolves, mermaids)

## Proposed Solution

Add 20 new fantasy styles organized into thematic groups. All prompts follow the established pattern:

1. **"Transform into..."** opening
2. **Detailed style/character description**
3. **Environment/setting** where relevant
4. **"Keep facial features recognizable"** preservation directive
5. **Technical specifications** (lighting, quality, atmosphere)

```

Dramatic lighting with golden hour glow. Battle-worn but heroic, determined expression. Chain mail visible at joints. Keep facial features recognizable. Hyper-realistic, cinematic, 8K quality.
```
